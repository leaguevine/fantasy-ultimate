Scoring rules

The scoring rules are very simple. Each fantasy player gets points awarded or deducted according to what they do during the games. Your fantasy team score is the sum of all your fantasy players' scores. Here are the rules for how points are awarded to fantasy players:

Event Points

Goal Caught +1
